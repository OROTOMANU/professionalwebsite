Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is a broad field that encompasses the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as roads, bridges, buildings, and water systems. It plays a crucial role in shaping the built environment and ensuring the safety and functionality of structures.

Roads & Bridges
Roads & Bridges engineering focuses on the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of transportation infrastructure, ensuring safe and efficient movement of people and goods.
Structural Engineering
Structural engineering involves the analysis and design of buildings, bridges, and other structures to ensure they can withstand loads and environmental forces safely.
Geotechnical Engineering
Geotechnical engineering deals with the behavior of earth materials and is essential for foundation design, slope stability, and soil-structure interaction.
Coastal Engineering
Coastal engineering focuses on managing coastal areas, including erosion control, harbor design, and protection against flooding and storm surges.
Environmental Engineering
Environmental engineering addresses issues related to water and air pollution, waste management, and sustainable development to protect human health and the environment.
Water Resources Engineering
Water resources engineering involves the management and distribution of water through dams, canals, pipelines, and treatment plants to ensure reliable water supply and flood control.
